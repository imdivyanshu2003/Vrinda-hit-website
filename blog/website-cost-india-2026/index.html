Option 1: DIY builders (Wix, Framer, Carrd)

Headline cost: "Free"

Real cost: ₹4,000 – ₹15,000/year plus 10–40 hours of your time learning the tool.

Free plans show the builder's ads and give you a URL like yourname.wixsite.com. To remove ads and add a custom domain, you're paying ₹400–₹1,200/month forever.

Hidden costs: premium templates (₹500–₹3,000), paid apps/plugins (₹200/month each), e-commerce features (+₹1,000/month).

Best for: hobbyists with unlimited time.

Option 2: Freelancers (₹5,000 – ₹25,000)

Cheapest end: a Fiverr designer will build a WordPress template site for ₹5,000. Mid-range: a skilled local freelancer will custom-design for ₹15,000–₹25,000.

Pros: real human, customization possible.

Cons:

  • Unpredictable timelines (7 days to 2 months)
  • Communication gaps
  • No post-launch support unless paid extra
  • Quality varies wildly

Option 3: Agencies (₹25,000 – ₹5,00,000)

Small Indian agencies start at ₹25,000 for a 5-page website. Larger, premium agencies in Bangalore/Mumbai/Delhi charge ₹1,00,000–₹5,00,000 for full branding + website.

Best for: funded startups, D2C brands, corporates.

Overkill for: creators, coaches, small service businesses.

The real hidden costs nobody mentions

1. Domain name

₹800–₹1,500/year for a .com. Cheaper for .in. Required if you want to look professional.

2. Hosting

₹3,000–₹15,000/year for shared hosting. Modern static sites (what we build) can be hosted free on Vercel or Netlify.

3. SSL certificate

Required for Google ranking and browser trust. Free with most modern hosts today — but some providers still charge ₹1,500/year.

4. Maintenance & updates

WordPress sites require ₹500–₹2,000/month in maintenance (security, plugin updates). Static sites need zero maintenance.

5. Content & images

Stock photos, copywriting, or photography can add ₹2,000–₹20,000. Or you can DIY with your phone and save this entirely.

Budget: ₹299 – ₹1,000 one-time. A single-page website with WhatsApp integration is all you need.

If you're serious (1,000–20,000 followers)

Budget: ₹999 – ₹3,000 total. Premium design + custom domain + basic SEO.

If you run a full service business

Budget: ₹2,000 – ₹10,000. Multi-page site, professional photography, SEO-optimized content.

When does an agency make sense?

Only if you have 6+ figure revenue already and need advanced features — inventory systems, custom bookings, integrations with CRMs. For 95% of creators and small businesses, an agency is massive overkill.

"You don't need a ₹50,000 website. You need a ₹1,000 website that converts."

Red flags to avoid

  • "Monthly fees forever." You should own your website, not rent it.
  • "We'll show you after payment." Always see a demo first.
  • "Quote depends on discussion." Fixed prices mean fixed scope.
  • Slow turnaround (>2 weeks) for a 1-page site. That's padding, not craftsmanship.
